XPRESS RP is a New Zealand based FiveM Economy server designed to be immersive and performant, XPRESS offers a wide range of activities and paths for your character to take, from joining emergency services to running your own trucking company or working in a lumberyard.

Designed and developed by FiveM veterans we make sure that every system that players encounter is polished and free of potential errors by the time it hits you, the end user.


All civilian, criminal and emergency activities have been tested, improved and intergrated with other scripts allowing for a cohesive experience where design philosophy is consistent.


We use only the best and most exploit resistant scripts possible keeping the playing field level for all players, XPRESS also only uses non gameplay altering monetisation such as priority queue.
